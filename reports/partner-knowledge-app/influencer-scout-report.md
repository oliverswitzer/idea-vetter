# Influencer Scout Report: Partner Knowledge App

**Date:** 2026-03-09
**App:** Partner Knowledge App — voice-narration + AI knowledge graph + personalized gift/date recommendations for men in committed relationships
**Target User:** Men 25-45 in committed relationships who want to be more thoughtful but lack a system
**Prepared by:** Influencer Scout Agent

---

## Methodology

YouTube Data API MCP tools were unavailable. All data was sourced from third-party analytics platforms (vidIQ, HypeAuditor, SPEAKRJ, SocialBlade, youtubers.me) via WebSearch and WebFetch. Stats reflect publicly available snapshots current as of early-to-mid 2026.

**Search queries used:**
1. "how to be a better boyfriend husband tips"
2. "gift ideas for girlfriend wife"
3. "men relationship advice dating"
4. "couples relationship tips advice"
5. "men self improvement lifestyle relationships"

**Channels scanned:** ~25 unique channels identified and evaluated.

**Engagement rate definition used:** (Average views per video / subscriber count) × 100. This measures how effectively a channel's subscribers actually watch each upload — a proxy for audience attentiveness and loyalty. Note: vidIQ also reports an "engagement score" based on likes+comments/views; both are noted where available.

**Filter thresholds applied:**
- Primary: engagement rate ≥ 10%, avg comments ≥ 100, at least 1 video in last 30 days
- Relaxed (applied with notation): engagement rate ≥ 5%, avg comments ≥ 50

**Audience fit disqualification:** Several channels with strong metrics were eliminated because their primary audience is women, not men. Mat Boggs (1.33M subs), Apollonia Ponti (709K), and Mindful Attraction 2.0 (448K) all coach women about men — wrong audience for this product.

**Niche disqualification:** DLM Men's Lifestyle (1.18M subs) was eliminated after discovering the channel has fully rebranded as "DLM Christian Perspective" in 2025, with content now focused entirely on biblical teaching and end-times prophecy.

**Inactivity flag:** Apollonia Ponti's most recent upload was February 3, 2026 — more than 30 days ago. Flagged as inactive under primary criteria.

## Summary

**Total channels scanned:** ~25 unique channels

**Total qualifying (primary filter, engagement ≥ 10%):** 1 clean qualifier (Stephan Speaks). Alpha M at 7.8% is borderline and included with a note.

**Relaxed filter applied:** Yes. Criteria relaxed to engagement ≥ 5% / avg comments ≥ 50 because fewer than 3 channels cleared primary thresholds with correct audience alignment.

**Total qualifying (relaxed + near-miss):** 5 channels.

---

### Top Niche Themes Observed

1. **Dating advice for single men** — the largest and most active subsector on YouTube (Tripp Advice, The Attractive Man). This is adjacent but not perfectly aligned. These creators' audiences are in earlier relationship stages, but the transition to "now I have a girlfriend and want to be thoughtful" is a natural pitch angle.

2. **Relationship advice for both genders** — Stephan Speaks sits here. Mixed male/female audience but a substantial male following who tune in to understand relationships. His comments reveal men actively discussing how to be better partners — high signal for this product.

3. **Men's lifestyle and self-improvement** — Alpha M and Charisma on Command. Neither is purely relationship-focused, but they serve the exact demographic: men 25-45 who want to level up. Alpha M explicitly covers relationship maintenance content alongside grooming and style.

4. **Gift guide content** — No dedicated YouTube channel found with a men's-oriented gift advice focus and significant engaged subscriber base. This niche exists on blog/affiliate sites but not as a sustained YouTube format.

---

### Recommended Priority Creators

**Priority 1: Stephan Speaks**
The single strongest match by raw metrics. His 12.1% engagement rate is exceptional, his 800 average comments per video signals a highly emotionally invested audience, and he posts 16 times per month meaning frequent integration opportunities. His content theme — helping men and women understand each other and build better relationships — is directly adjacent to the app's pitch. The downside: his audience skews 40-60% female. However, his male viewers are exactly the type who are actively working on relationships rather than just dating. A sponsored segment framing the app as "a system for being the partner she deserves" would resonate in his feed. Contact via stephan@stephanspeaks.com.

**Priority 2: Tripp Advice**
Despite the 4.6% recent engagement rate, Tripp Advice is the most tightly focused on the male target user. His audience is men who are actively trying to improve in relationships. His 1.08M subscribers and ~150+ estimated comments per video shows an engaged base. The pitch angle: "You've figured out how to attract someone — now here's how to keep them happy without having to remember everything." This is a natural upsell moment in the relationship journey his audience is on. Outreach via the contact form at trippadvice.com/contact-us/ (no public email found).

**Priority 3: Alpha M**
The largest male audience on this list at 6.92M subscribers. Alpha M covers style, grooming, fitness, and relationships — and explicitly publishes content titled "Tips to Keep Your Relationship Fresh." His audience is men 25-40 actively investing in self-improvement, which maps directly to the app's core user. His per-video views (~542K average) mean a single mention reaches a massive audience. The engagement rate at 7.8% is solid for his scale. His email is not publicly listed; outreach likely goes through alpham.com's brand partnership page.

**Stretch Candidate: Charisma on Command**
Not a pure relationship channel, but his 2,000+ average comments per video is the highest on this list and signals rare audience depth. His men's self-improvement framing — "be more confident, communicate better, build stronger connections" — is upstream of the product's value. An integration framing the app as a confidence system for relationships (never blank on what she said she wanted again) could land well. His low posting frequency (4-6 per month) means fewer integration points, and his audience is broader, but the raw reach at 6.96M and comment depth justify outreach exploration.

**Skip for now: Coach Corey Wayne**
Despite a perfectly aligned niche (men's relationship self-improvement), his ~20 average comments per video against 604K subscribers reveals a passive audience that watches without engaging. Sponsored content in a low-engagement environment tends to underperform. His 56 videos per month also signals content volume over depth — audiences may be fatigued. Not recommended for initial outreach.
