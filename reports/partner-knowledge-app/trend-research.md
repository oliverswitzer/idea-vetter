## Consumer Behavior

- Average annual gift spending: $1,000-1,200/person
- Partners are #1 gift recipient (60%+ of consumers)
- 88% struggle with gift ideas
- 60% say gift culture has gotten "out of hand"
- 48% report gift fatigue
- Growing preference for experiential gifts over physical
- 40%+ YoY surge in "personalized gifts for girlfriend" searches

## Key Tension

Highest-value moments (gift/date occasions) happen 4-5x/year, but the app needs year-round engagement to build the knowledge base. This is the core product design challenge.
