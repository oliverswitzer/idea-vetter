## Cross-App Review Complaints (ranked by frequency)

1. **Paywall frustration** — Free tiers too limited (Paired, Couple Joy, Evergreen, Couple Game)
2. **Content runs out** — Finite question pools exhausted by long-term users
3. **Not designed for in-person use** — Apps assume partners are apart
4. **Billing confusion** — Double charges, unclear trial conversion
5. **Limited personalization** — Questions feel generic, not tailored
6. **Feels like homework** — Clinical rather than organic
7. **No actionable output** — Data collected but nothing useful done with it

## Cross-App Praise (ranked by frequency)

1. **Sparks real conversations** — Best value is post-app discussion
2. **Discover new things about long-term partner** — Even 25-30 year couples learn something
3. **Good for LDR** — Fills connection gap
4. **Simple daily habit** — 5 min/day works
5. **Beautiful design** — UI quality matters in this category

## Feature Requests From Reviews

1. Better use of accumulated answer data — users want insights and trends
2. More personalized/contextual questions
3. Gift and date ideas tied to what partner actually likes
4. Progress tracking over time
5. Ability to retake quizzes and see change
6. In-person date night mode

## Pricing Landscape

- Couple apps: $5-$15/mo or $25-$75/yr (standard freemium)
- Between offers lifetime at $26.99
- Gift apps: mostly free (Giftful, Gift Suggester)
- Gift subscription apps largely fail (GiftPal at $99.99/yr has 3 ratings)

## Key Gaps Identified

1. **No app bridges "Know Your Partner" and "Act On It"** — Data flows into a void
2. **No persistent, growing partner profile** — Lovewick's is manual notes
3. **AI gift apps are stateless** — Re-enter details every time
4. **Voice input absent** — No app uses voice for partner knowledge
5. **Date recs not personalized** — Generic filters only
6. **Gift + relationship apps completely siloed**
