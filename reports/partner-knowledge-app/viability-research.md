# Viability Research: Partner Knowledge App

**Research Date:** 2026-03-06

## Competitor Landscape

### Direct Competitors (Couples Apps)

**Lovewick** — Closest existing competitor
- "Forget-Me-Nots" feature: partner detail storage (favorites, sizes, dates, allergies)
- 750+ date ideas, discovery cards, wishlists, relationship timeline
- Pricing: Free / Plus at $29.99/yr, $14.99/qtr, $9.99/mo
- 4.9 stars Apple App Store, 300K+ users, $1M pre-seed
- Forget-me-nots is manual text entry — no voice, no AI, no knowledge graph, no gift recs

**Paired** — Market leader
- 8M downloads, Google Play Awards 2020 winner
- Daily questions, quizzes, therapist-led content
- No partner knowledge base or gift recommendations
- Pricing: $59.99/yr or $14.99/mo; ~$200K/mo revenue

**Flamme** — AI-focused
- 150K+ couples, Pre-Seed funded
- AI Relationship Coach for gifts, intimacy, conflict
- Daily questions, date planner, shared bucket list

**Between** — Private messaging
- 35M couples historically
- Private chat, shared photos, anniversary reminders
- $2.99/mo, $13.99/yr, $26.99 lifetime

**Couply** — AI-powered quizzes
- 280K downloads, 4.47 rating
- AI coach, love language/attachment/MBTI quizzes
- $15/mo or ~$103/yr

### AI Gift Tools

**GyftPro** (Dec 2025) — "Relationship-aware AI" analyzing connection context. Closest gift competitor but no persistent profiles.

**Giftly** (web) — Builds profile from social media + past purchases with ML. Closest to persistent learning but web-only, not partner-specific.

**GiftStar AI, Giftruly, DreamGift, Outdone** — All stateless one-shot query tools.

**Giftful** — Wishlist/registry app, 4.9 stars, 65K ratings, FREE. Requires partner to maintain list.

### Personal CRM

**Monica** — 24.3K GitHub stars, open-source. Manages MANY relationships, not deep on ONE person. Praised by people with Asperger's/Alzheimer's.

## Pain Point Evidence

### Forgetting Anniversaries
- Dating.com survey: 82% have forgotten anniversary, 82% of forgetters male, 68% would break up over it
- Therapist Anita Chlipala: repeated forgetting "could be indicative of something more problematic"

### Gift Selection Struggle
- 88% struggle with gift ideas (GiftAFeeling 2025)
- Nearly 1 in 5 think spouse is "disappointing" at gifts
- Psychology Today: gifts are "a barometer for your relationship"

### Manual Tracking Behavior (Behavioral Validation)
- Viral TikTok trend: men keeping Notes app lists about partners (#rulesfordating 771K+ views)
- Reddit viral story: man's "gift ideas" note about wife — universally praised
- Obsidian forum: detailed relationship templates (likes, dislikes, attachment style, personality type)

## Creepiness Risk Assessment

**Creepy framing:** HN users on Monica: "This FEELS ungenuine"; Obsidian user: "If I knew someone would write down every little detail about me, I would be weirded out"

**Sweet framing:** TikTok trend overwhelmingly positive; Reddit universally praised; Monica supporters: "It's my own very private way of keeping people in my life"

**Key insight:** Same behavior, different perception based on framing. "Love journal" = sweet. "Partner database" = creepy. Voice narration adds intimacy but also surveillance vibes. Privacy (on-device) is critical.

## Key Gaps

1. No app bridges "Know Your Partner" and "Act On It"
2. No persistent, growing AI partner profile
3. Voice input for partner knowledge doesn't exist
4. Contextual date/gift recs based on actual partner data don't exist
5. Gifting + relationship app categories are completely siloed
